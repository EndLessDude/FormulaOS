Welcome

FormulaOS is an Operating System that runs on a browser and is built around the theme of Formula 1. I made it with this theme due to my love for the sport.

The wallpaper features the Mercedes driver, George Russel doing the famous T-Pose during the 2025 F1 intro.

Apps:

F1 Quiz: A 5 question quiz that tests your knowledge about Formula 1.

Lights Out: A game that tests your reation time in a motorsport fashion.

Champions: A table showing all 35 F1 world champions.

F1 2026 Calendar: A visual calendar of the 2026 F1 season.

2026 F1 Cars: A showcase of every car on the 2026 grid.
